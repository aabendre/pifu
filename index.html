Plan It For You: AKA Pifu

Our mission is to make your life simple by taking over planning and leaving just the fun part for you!

We will ask some simple questions to get your planning needs and then plan your vacation, event or business related meet and greet/ travel for you. You can customize the searches, provide preferances which will help train our algorithms for better searches next time. Power of AI! You can provide prompts as changes or midfications need to happen. We will monitor airline prices, suggest when is a right time to book, if its too early, too late based on historical trends and get you the best price, so you don't have to go and navigate mulitple websites and constantly compare and contrast. We can co-ordinate schedules and provide group booking options. Places to stay, either a rental or a hotel will also be suggested based on your preferances, so again you don't need to go on mulieple websites to look at a million options.
